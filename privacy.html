2. Hosting at Hetzner

3. Server log files

When you access the site, Hetzner automatically records information that your browser transmits (server log files), in particular:

  • IP address (stored shortened or pseudonymised where configured)
  • Date and time of request
  • Time zone difference to GMT
  • Requested content (specific page/file)
  • HTTP status code
  • Transferred data volume
  • Referrer URL
  • Browser type/version and operating system

Processing is necessary for the technical delivery, stability and security of the website (e.g. to defend against attacks) and is based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Log data are deleted automatically once no longer required (usually after a few days to weeks).

4. Cookies and tracking

This website uses no cookies requiring consent and no analytics or tracking tools. The image lightbox works purely with HTML/CSS and stores no data.

5. Website content & media

Images are delivered from our own server (Hetzner). No external CDNs or third-party services are embedded. Fonts are provided by the system or locally; no external web fonts are requested.

11. Your rights

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)

  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)

You can exercise your rights by contacting us using the contact details above.
